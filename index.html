This calculator is for adults only (pregnant or breastfeeding women should not rely on BMI calculations). No action should be taken based on the result other than consulting a qualified professional, such as a doctor.


The calculator gives you an overview of your weight comparison using the Body Mass Index (BMI), which is calculated by dividing your weight (in kilograms) by the square of your height (in meters): BMI = Kg/MÂ².



BMI does not fully represent a comprehensive health diagnosis or disease risk. Further consultation is needed to address concerns and risks related to your weight.
